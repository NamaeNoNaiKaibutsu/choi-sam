Choi Sam

Choi Sam, born Choi Ji-ae, is a South Korean rapper
who debuted under Kwang Music in 2015.
She has released 2 studio albums, 7 mixtapes, 2 EPs
and numerous singles.
Throughout her songs she uses a big variety of flows
and her distinctive voice gives her music originality.
Her nickname is Black Mamba.
Her favorite food is lemons.
Some of her favorite western rappers are Jay-Z and
Nas. She has used samples from both of these artists
songs.
